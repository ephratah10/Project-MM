Beginner's Program

Tailored for newcomers, this program covers the basics of firearm safety, handling, and marksmanship. It's the perfect starting point for anyone looking to get into shooting sports.

Competitive Shooting Program

For those looking to test their skills, our competitive program offers training and entry into local, regional, and national competitions. Focuses include precision rifle, pistol shooting, and 3-gun competitions.

Our Work

At Mist's Range, our core mission is to promote responsible gun ownership and shooting sports. We pride ourselves on offering top-notch training programs, well-maintained shooting lanes, and a variety of firearms for rental. Our team of certified instructors is dedicated to providing personalized coaching, ensuring that safety and skill development are paramount. Through community engagement and education, we strive to foster a positive shooting culture that respects tradition and embraces progress
